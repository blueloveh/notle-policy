# Pillog Privacy Policy

**Effective date: June 12, 2026**

Pillog ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the minimum information needed to provide the service.

- **Account information**: email address, password (stored encrypted); when you use Sign in with Apple, the identifier and email Apple provides.
- **Content you enter**: member name/color/emoji, supplement details (generic name, product name, brand, form, ingredients, dosage, expiry date, memo), supplement photos, dosing schedules, and intake records (taken/skipped status and time).
- **On-device storage**: your login session token and notification reminder identifiers (used to reschedule reminders). This data is stored only on your device and is not sent to our servers.

The App does **not** collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or analytics/tracking data.

## 2. How We Use Information

- Authenticate you and keep you signed in
- Manage supplements and schedules, and deliver time-of-day intake reminders
- Provide statistics such as adherence and history
- Export your data (JSON/CSV) at your request

## 3. Local Notifications

Reminders are **local notifications scheduled and delivered on your device only.** The App does not collect push tokens or send notifications from a server.

## 4. Data Storage and Processing

- App data is stored with **Supabase** (a cloud Postgres database and file storage), which acts as our data processor.
- All data is isolated with Row-Level Security (RLS) so that **only you** can access your own data, and data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS.

## 5. Sharing With Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information and do not share it with third parties other than the infrastructure provider (Supabase) described in Section 4. The App contains no advertising.

## 6. Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active and is deleted without undue delay when you delete your data or request account deletion. You can export your data at any time from the Settings screen.

## 7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, deletion of, or export of your personal information. To delete your account or data, contact us at the address below.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 14 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you enter a child's intake information as a family member, you, as the guardian, are responsible for that information.

## 9. Medical Disclaimer

The timing and ingredient-combination guidance provided by the App is **general reference information and not medical advice.** Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking any supplement.

## 10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including Row-Level Security (RLS), encryption in transit (HTTPS), and device secure storage.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will notify you within the App or on this page.
