Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the difficulty?
On the Home screen, tap the difficulty selector before starting a new game. You can choose from Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert, Master, and Legend.
What happens when I reach 3 mistakes?
If the Mistake Limit is turned on in Settings, the game ends after 3 mistakes. You can disable this limit in Settings → Gameplay → Mistake Limit to play without a penalty.
What are Hints and how many can I use?
Tap the 💡 Hint button during a game to reveal the correct number for the selected cell. There is no hard limit on hints, but using more hints lowers your final score and XP reward.
How is my score calculated?
Your score is based on the puzzle difficulty, time taken, number of mistakes, and hints used. Solving a harder puzzle faster with no mistakes and no hints gives the highest score.
What is the Day Streak?
Your streak increases each day you complete at least one puzzle. Missing a day resets your streak back to zero, so try to play daily!
How do I use Note mode?
Tap the ✎ Notes button to toggle Note mode on. While active, tapping a number on the keypad adds it as a small candidate note in the selected cell. Tap Notes again to return to normal input mode.
My progress was lost. Can I recover it?
Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, progress cannot be recovered. We recommend not deleting the app to preserve your stats.
How do I change the app theme?
Go to Settings (tap the gear icon on the Home screen) and scroll down to App Theme. Choose from Aurora, Cosmos, Grove, or Latte.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at blueloveh03@gmail.com with a description of the bug and your device model and iOS version. Screenshots are very helpful!
